# VMS Master Strategy

> Strategic playbook for visitormanagement.us — the USA/global market brand of Vizitor.

---

## 1. Strategic Context

### Where We Stand
- **Current ARR:** ~$265K (growing)
- **Monthly traffic (vizitorapp.com India site):** ~3,700 visits
- **Indexed pages:** 1,068 (India site)
- **Blog posts:** 150+ (India site)
- **Market share:** <0.02% of $2B VMS TAM

### The Opportunity
- VMS market: $2.0–2.18B (2026), growing to $6.98B by 2035 (13.8% CAGR)
- Workplace management software: $3.02B → $10.30B by 2035
- Queue management: $0.6–1.07B → $1.20B by 2032
- Combined addressable market: $60–73B (workplace tech)

### Why a Separate US Brand
The India site (vizitorapp.com) serves the Indian market well. **visitormanagement.us** is a new, separate brand for the US/global market because:
1. **Exact-match domain** — "visitor management" is literally in the domain
2. **USD pricing** — $36/mo positions against Envoy ($109+), not Indian competitors
3. **US-centric trust signals** — SOC 2, HIPAA references, US-focused case studies
4. **Clean SEO start** — no cannibalization with the India site

**Estimated impact:** 50–80 programmatic pages → 10,000–30,000 monthly organic visitors within 6–12 months.
