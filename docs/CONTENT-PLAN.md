## Blog Post Template

Every blog post should follow this structure:

```markdown
---
title: "Post Title"
slug: "post-slug"
description: "Meta description (150-160 chars)"
date: "2026-XX-XX"
author: "VMS Team"
pillar: "queue-management" | "visitor-management" | etc.
keywords: ["primary keyword", "secondary keyword"]
---

## Introduction (100-150 words)
Hook + problem statement + what the reader will learn.

## H2 Sections (main content)
Structured with clear H2s that match search queries.
Include data, examples, and actionable advice.

## How VMS Helps (product tie-in)
Natural connection to our product — not salesy, but relevant.

## FAQ Section
3-5 questions in Q&A format (enables FAQ schema).

## Conclusion + CTA
Summary + link to demo/free trial.
```

### Word Count Targets
- Standard posts: 1,500–2,000 words
- Pillar/guide posts: 2,500–3,000 words
- Comparison posts: 2,000–2,500 words

- Link to pillar page: Required
